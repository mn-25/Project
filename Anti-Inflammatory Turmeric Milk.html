Anti-Inflammatory Turmeric Milk
PREP TIME:
3 minutes
COOK TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
1 serving
The reason I said this milk will become your BFF, is because you will start to feel the turmeric powder immediately. After just a few sips, you will get a natural buzz similar to that of drinking a cup of coffee. Yes, please!

If you continue to drink this milk daily, I promise that you will begin to notice a difference in energy, complexion, hair/nails... EVERYTHING. And these aren't even the major benefits I listed earlier!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 cup almond milk, unsweetened (or milk of choice)
  • 1 cup filtered water
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/4 inch fresh ginger root, peeled
  • pinch of, black pepper
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and puree until smooth.

    2. Add the milk to a small pot and heat over medium for approximately 5 minutes until the milk begins to boil. Remove from heat. Drink warm.
