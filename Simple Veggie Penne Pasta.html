Simple Veggie Penne Pasta
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
COOK TIME:
10 minutes
SERVINGS:
6 servings
ATTN: CALLING ALL PASTA LOVERS.

I've got a new one for you guys, and I think you're going to like it.

It is so simple ant absolutly delisious! Enjoy!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 16 ounces brown rice penne pasta
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sun dried tomatoes
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced or diced
  • 5 cups spinach
  • to taste, red chili pepper flakes
  • to taste, salt and pepper
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Cook pasta according to directions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.

    2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive olive oil in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add garlic cloves and cook for 2-3 minutes or until fragrant. Add sun dried tomatoes and bell peppers. Bring to medium heat. Cook for approximately 5 minutes until the bell peppers are tender. Carefully stir in spinach. Season with salt and pepper as desired.

    3. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet of veggies. Toss to cover the pasta with olive oil and to mix the veggies into the pasta well. Sprinkle with red chili pepper flakes and more salt and pepper as desired. Serve immediately.
