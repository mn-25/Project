Fresh Strawberry Mojito (Non-Alcoholic Option)
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
1 Strawberry Mojito
Fresh Strawberry Mojitos that are delicious, fresh, and naturally sweetened with berries and a bit of agave! These can be enjoyed with or without the addition of rum and make for the most perfect refreshing cocktail, or mocktail!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 fresh strawberries
  • 5-7 mint leaves
  • 1 - 1/2 tablespoons agave nectar
  • 1/2 lime, juice of
  • ice cubes
  • 1 ounce clear rum optional
  • soda water
  • extra strawberries for garnishing
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. In a tall glass, add strawberries, mint, and agave nectar. Muddle ingredients until well mashed. Be sure to only press the ingredients while muddling. Do not grind them up. (Otherwise you will have little pieces of mint getting stuck in your teeth!). Squeeze in the lime juice. Then add rum if you are using it. Add ice cubes. Fill the remainder of the glass with soda water. Garnish with a strawberry and enjoy!
