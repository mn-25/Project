Quick & Easy Pesto Tortellini Pasta Salad
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
COOK TIME:
20 minutes
SERVINGS:
8 servings
This Quick & Easy Pesto Tortellini Pasta Salad is delicious! Itâ€™s perfect for family get-togethers, as a side dish, or meal-prepped and used for busy weekday lunches.
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 - 16 ounce package dry tortellini of choice
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small purple onion, chopped
  • 2/3 cup kalmata olives, whole or sliced
  • 1 -14 ounce can of artichoke hearts, drained and cut into fourths
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce
  • fresh basil, to garnish
  • cracked pepper, to garnish
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and season with salt. Add the tortellini and cook according to directions on the package. Usually about 16-18 minutes. Once the tortellini is cooked and tender, turn off the heat and add a cup of cold water to the pot and then strain.

    2. While the tortellini is in the strainer, rinse it with cold water for 1-2 minutes to cool it. Transfer the tortellini to a large bowl.

    3. Add the tomatoes, onions, olives, artichoke hearts, and pesto to the bowl of tortellini and toss well until all the ingredients are well combined.

    4. Garnish with fresh basil and a pinch of black pepper. Enjoy immediately or store in the fridge for 1-2 hours before serving.
