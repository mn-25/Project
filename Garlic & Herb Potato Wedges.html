Garlic & Herb Potato Wedges w/ 2-Ingredient Chipotle Dipping Sauce
PREP TIME:
10 minutes
COOK TIME:
30 minutes
SERVINGS:
4-5 servings
These potato wedges are a great addition to the party-food table. It's an easy-to-make finger food that is generally loved across the board. Plus you'll be serving them with an addicting chipotle sauce, which by the way, is simply Sriracha and vegan mayo mixed together. But nobody needs to know that, am I right?

I think I've rambled enough for you today, so just know that these potato wedges are really great for snacking and are a healthy option to pair with your burgers.
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 4 russet potatoes, washed and cut into wedges
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • pinch of, salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon cilantro, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Sriracha
  • 2 tablespoons vegan mayonaise (like "Just Mayo")
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line and oil a baking sheet. Set aside.

    2. Add the wedges to a large bowl. Pour in olive oil and toss to coat.

    3. In a small container, stir to mix the seasoning ingredients. Toss the wedges with the seasoning mixture. Sprinkle with cilantro.

    4. Lay the wedges flat on the baking sheet, skin side down. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.

    5. 2-INGREDIENT CHIPOTLE DIPPING SAUCE: Mix the Sriracha and mayo together until well combined.
