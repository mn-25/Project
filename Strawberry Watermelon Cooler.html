Strawberry Watermelon Cooler
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
2 servings
The combination of watermelon, strawberries, and lime juice gives this juice a refreshing and tangy flavor. Toss in some ginger root for spice and a little maple syrup for a sweet and cooling factor. And to top it all off, add some chia seeds for an extra healthy boost.
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 cups fresh watermelon
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons chia seeds
  • 1 inch ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Add all ingredients to your blender and blend until smooth. Pour over ice and serve immediately.
