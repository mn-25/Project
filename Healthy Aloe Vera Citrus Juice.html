Healthy Aloe Vera Citrus Juice
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
1 serving
This Aloe Vera Citrus Juice keeps you hydrated, promotes healthy digestion, and gives you a boost of vitamin c! All you have to do is toss a few ingredients in the blender and you are good to go! Fresh, delicious, and healthy. Enjoy!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 oranges, peel removed
  • 3-4 strawberries
  • 1/4 cup filtered water
  • 1/4 cup aloe vera juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Add all ingredients to your blender and puree until smooth. Enjoy as is or serve over ice.
