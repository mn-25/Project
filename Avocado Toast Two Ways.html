Avocado Toast Two Ways
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
1 serving
Avocado & Tomato Toast: This is typically my go to. I love savory foods and this hits the spot for me every single time. I usually add ground flax seed too, for added health perks.

It's got a thick layer of avocado, tomato slices, a generous amount of my favorite hot sauce, and red chili pepper flakes. IT'S S'GOOD!

Strawberry & Balsamic Toast: This is my preference when I've got just a bit of a sweet tooth or if I'm craving something refreshing and "cooling".

This version has a hearty amount of avocado, sweet strawberry slices, chopped spinach, and balsamic vinegar (which I'm obsessed with).

No matter what breakfast makes you happy, I encourage you to use it as a way to transform your mornings into a time that you really cherish. Because it really has increased the positivity in my life!

INGREDIENTS
  • 2 slices bread, toasted
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/2 tomato, sliced
  • dash hot sauce
  • to taste red chili pepper flakes
  • to taste salt and pepper
  • 1-2 strawberries, sliced
  • 2-3 spinach leaves, chopped
  • drizzle balsamic vinegar
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    Avocado & Tomato Toast: Layer in the following order - toast, avocado, tomato, hot sauce, chili pepper, salt & pepper.

    Strawberry & Balsamic Toast: Layer in the following order - toast, avocado, spinach, strawberries, balsamic vinegar
