Vegan Hot and Cheesy Corn Dip
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
COOK TIME:
35 minutes
SERVINGS:
6-8 servings
This Hot and Cheesy Corn Dip is the ultimate crowd pleaser! An old-school party dish revamped and made vegan using dairy-free jack cheese, dairy-free mayonnaise, and nutritional yeast. Mix that together with corn and green chilis, bake, and serve with your favorite crackers or chips. It's quick, easy, and delicious!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 2 cups corn, drained and rinsed if canned or thawed if frozen
  • 1 - 4 ounce can diced green chilis
  • 1/2 cup dairy-free mayonnaise (I used Follow Your Heart brand)
  • 1 - 8 ounce package of dairy-free jack cheese, divided
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
  • cilantro, for garnishing
  • fresh jalapeño slices, for garnishing
  • fresh jalapeño slices, for garnishing
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil a 9-inch baking dish and set aside.

    2. In a mixing bowl, add all remaining ingredients (except for 1/2 cup of jack cheese) and stir until thoroughly mixed.

    3. Now, sprinkle the reserved 1/2 cup of cheese on the top of the dip. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until slightly golden. Garnish with cilantro and jalapeños if desired. Best served warm with crackers or chips!
