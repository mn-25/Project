Healthy Homemade Pumpkin Hummus
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
Healthy Homemade Pumpkin Hummus. A festive spin on traditional hummus. You will love the subtle sweetness of pumpkin and cinnamon. Serve with pita bread, pretzels, or crackers. Enjoy!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 can pumpkin puree
  • 1 - 15oz. can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Throw all ingredients into your food processor or high power blender and pulse until smooth. Serve with some pita bread and dig in.

    2. Store in an air tight container in the refrigerator.
