Festive Summer Fruit Salad
PREP TIME:
15 minutes
SERVINGS:
5 servings
This Festive Summer Fruit Salad is the ultimate summer staple. Perfect for BBQâ€™s, brunch, or a day at the beach. Made using only ingredients from mother nature - it does not get more fresh than this!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 watermelon, cut in half
  • fresh fruit of choice ( I prefer berries and kiwi)
  • coconut flakes
  • fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • lime wedges
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Cut the watermelon in half a scoop out the inside. We will use the hollow watermelon as a bowl! Take the watermelon that you scooped out, and cut it into bite size pieces.

    2. Fill the empty watermelon bowl with the cut up watermelon, berries, kiwi, and any other fruit you like.

    3. Sprinkle with fresh mint leaves and coconut flakes. Serve lime wedges on the side so people can squeeze it on their salad if desired. Enjoy!
