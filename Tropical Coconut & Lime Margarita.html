Tropical Coconut & Lime Margarita
PREP TIME:
5 minutes
SERVINGS:
3-4 servings
These Tropical Coconut & Lime Margaritas will make you feel like you’re on island time! You only need a few simple ingredients to whip them up - coconut cream, lime juice, agave nectar, and your favorite blanco tequila. Don’t want the alcohol? Skip the tequila and make it a delicious mocktail. Cheers!
print this recipe
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 - 13.5 ounce canned coconut cream
  • 6 tablespoons lime juice (about 2-3 limes)
  • 3 tablespoons agave nectar
  • 4.5 ounces blanco tequila (about 3 shots)
  • salt or sugar, to rim the glasses
    • INSTRUCTIONS
    1. Grab a couple of rocks or margarita glasses and rub the rims with a lime wedge, this will help salt or sugar stick to the rim. Tip: I recommend a sugar rim for coconut margaritas. I used a hibiscus sugar.

    2. Pour a bit of salt or sugar (whichever you’re using) onto a small plate. Run the rim of the glasses through the salt/sugar. Set the glasses aside.

    3. Add the coconut cream, lime juice, agave nectar, and tequila to your blender and give it a quick blend to mix all of the ingredients. You can also do this in a shaker if you prefer.

    4. Fill the rimmed glasses with ice and distribute the coconut margarita mixture evenly into the glasses. This recipe should make about 3-4 margaritas depending on the size of your glasses. Cheers!
